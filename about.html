About Meself

and my Colorful Stories

Year of 2002

In 2002, a baby in the name of Dung was born in a hospital, far from his hometown, in the city called Hanoi, which is the capital of Vietnam. His mother was a teacher in Thaibinh province and his dad was a salesman. Dung was later renamed to Hoang, because coincidentally enough, one of his great uncle happened to be named "Dung" as well, which was a no no at the time. Hoang was a healthy boy with a natural talent of using his left hand. His family later moved to Hanoi, a city of opportunities.

Year of 2013

As the year of 2013 rolled around, he who was mourning in the lost of his grandfather, who he dearly loved, began his journey into the wilderness of middle school. Hoang, who was short and skinny, got along very well with his classmates and became one of the most liked people in class. BUT!, his joy quickly came to a halt when his Math teacher turned out to be his own mother. His nightmares began.

2013-2017

Many things can happen in 4 years, and puperty was one of them. Hoang has grown up to become a fairly strong, not so tall, not so handsome of a man, and many experience with love. Throughout those years, thanks to his mother's teaching and occasional nagging, maths became one of his strong suits, helping him ace his pre-highschool exams, one of the most important exams in Vietnam. Hoang began flipping to a new chapters, studying at one of the most prestigious high school in the city.

Year of 2018

4 months have passed since his first day in the new teritory, Hoang began packing up and saying goodbye to his friends and family to start a new life, with a new high school in Canada. Hoang was so excited, yet scared of what is ahead of him. Nevertheless, Hoang headed into the new page with his head up high and never looked back.

2018-2020

High school in Canada has finally come to an end, but not what Hoang had expected. He managed to make many good friends, get used to the life of a Canadian citizen, and was having the best time of his life. Covid happened.............. And you know the rest. Many sleepless nights sitting on computer, wasting away, waiting for this pandemic to finally end. His life was flipped upside down.

2020-2021

Hoang headed into university with a bit of a depression. Everything seemed to go in a wrong direction, and Hoang found himself stumbling in every step. Luckily, a person has stepped back into Hoang's life, an old buddy from work, and shaked things up in a positive way. He and Hoang are best friends till this day.

Present - The Finale

Hoang is now entering his third year of university, with a much better mentality and a brider future. He is now following his brother's footstep of becoming a Web Developer, starting from Front End to later on, Back End.

His journey of going Full Stack begins now.
